> __**Planted Feet**__
⬥ Removes the DoT of sun/swiftness but extend the duration to 37s allowing you to use more thresholds in your ultimates
⬥ Priority: `Very High`
_ _
.
> __**Aftershock 3 (+ Combos)**__
⬥ Afterhock 3 - Every 50k damage aftershock can proc for an AoE blast dealing 72-118.8% (average 95.4%) abil damage
⬥ Priority: `High`

⬥ Aftershock 3 | Invigorating 1 on Staff, Aftershock 3 | Precise 1 on other things, done for a marginal dps increase
⬥ Priority: `Low`

⬥ Aftershock 1/2 | Planted Feet, done in order to not lose aftershock stacks when using Sun/DS
⬥ Priority: `High`

⬥ Aftershock 1 | Equilibrium 2, done solely for sgb, so you can have p5 on it for the spec and not have to 1 tick the spec to maintain stacks (also so you can apply BSA stacks if need be)
⬥ Priority: `High if using SGB`
_ _
.
> __**Precise/Equilibrium Combo**__
⬥ These just increase your overall average damage by a set amount due to increase min and decreasing max
⬥ Biting helps increase their average dps increase

⬥ P3E2 is an ~6% dps boost assuming Biting 0, where P5 is marginally better but more expensive
⬥ Priority: `High`

⬥ P4E2 is ~1% dps boost over P3E2
⬥ Priority: `High`

⬥ P5E1 is a marginal boost over P4E2 and is BiS for melee, range, and dw magic
⬥ Priority: `Low`
_ _
.
> __**Flank 3**__
⬥ Only works from the back/sides of creatures, removing their stunning ability for increased damage
⬥ Turns backhand, impact and binding shot from 100% basics to 220% basics
⬥ Turns forceful backhand, deep impact and tight bindings from 200% thresholds to 290% thresholds
⬥ Generally limited to group pvming content
⬥ Priority: `High if you can flank boss, Low otherwise`
_ _
.
> __**Lunging 3**__
⬥ Increases max hit of Dismember, Fragmentation Shot and Combust by +60% if not walked
⬥ Generally only used with melee as you cannot walk Dismember and Frag Shot + Combust are better if walked
⬥ Priority: `High if melee, Low otherwise`

> __**Armour Perks**__
.tag:armour
_ _
.
> __**Biting 3**__
⬥ Gives a 6% (6.6% at level 20) chance of forcing a crit each hit
⬥ Priority: `High`
_ _
.
> __**Crackling 3 (+ Combos)**__
⬥ Crackling 3 deals 150% ability damage every 60 seconds
      • Note: This can snipe Ingenuity of the Humans sigil ability.
⬥ Can be comboed with several things, the most common being Mobile, however there are other things like the X Slayer perks
⬥ Priority: `High`
_ _
.
> __**Enhanced Devoted 3**__
⬥ 13.5% (14.85% at level 20) chance per hit on you of activating a 3 second version of the Devotion ability
⬥ This takes priority over the normal Devoted perk, however the normal Devoted perk can activate during the time Enhanced devotion is active
⬥ The average reduction is highly dependant on the attack speed and rate you are being hit by the enemy/enemies
⬥ Priority: `Medium`
      • Note: More of a filler perk, can replace if you have a better perk to use and do not really require the damage reduction
_ _
.
> __**Impatient 3**__
⬥ Has a 27% chance to grant 3% more adren for a basic
⬥ On average this means an ~0.8 increase in adrenaline per basic
⬥ Priority: `Medium`
_ _
.
> __**Combination Slayer perks**__
⬥ There is one for Demon, Dragons and Undead
⬥ Each one provides a 7% boost in damage assuming the creature is of the same type as the slayer perk
⬥ Does work on some bosses
⬥ Priority: `High if you can use it (e.g. Elite Dungeons 2/3), Low otherwise`
